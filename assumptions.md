###### Source of non-standard unit conversion factors: 



Where available conversion factors were sourced from the Malawi IHS4. Data on non-standard units was collected from North, Central and South Malawi during the survey. An average of the conversion factors from these 3 regions was calculated and then matched to food items in the Rwanda EICV7.

For some food items, conversion factors for equivalent foods were not available in the Malawi IHS4, in these cases the conversion factors were borrowed from either the Tanzania NPS, or the Senegal EHCVM.

Relevant excel sheets and csv's available in metadata folder.


Data on non-standard units for the Rwanda EICV7 has been requested from the National Institute of Statistics Rwanda (NISR). Still awaited.
